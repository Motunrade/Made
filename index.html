Welcome to Made International Schools.

In the city and surrounding neighborhoods of Lagos, Nigeria, there are very few quality options for secondary education, locally know as Junior Secondary School (JSS) 1-3 and Senior Secondary School (SSS) 1-3. This results in the families of Lagos having to send their young children to schools hundreds, if not thousands, of miles away to receive quality education. Navigating unfamiliar environments has always been a key part of a education, whether the challenge is physical, important or leadership.

OUR NURSERY SCHOOL

Our Young Pre classroom is for ages 2+. This age group is working on developing their fine and gross motor skills, that are gaining independence, and developing their social skills through play.

OUR PRIMARY SCHOOL

As children move through Nursery School to Primary, the emphasis is on building a foundation for lifelong learning and community service. Expectations for academic achievement are set high, yet balanced with a rich curriculum of competitive and recreational athletics, music and drama, visual arts and exciting excursions. They continue to gain confidence and build the resilience and strength of character that they need to take smart risks, use their voice and develop to their full potential.

OUR SECONDARY SCHOOL

We are a mixed secondary for all young people in the local area. We are a true community school that involves all in the community in every aspect of what we do. We believe that every child can achieve great things when supported by excellent teachers, a challenging curriculum and engaged families. Everyone at Made International Schools is Valued. We care deeply about our students and our staff. We do all we can to ensure each pupil is known, that they are happy and they make progress in their learning based on our knowledge of how they best learn. We are highly inclusive and very successful with students that are on track or those that need significant support from our excellent team. This is our first promise.
